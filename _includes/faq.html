Is this for me?

A few frequently asked questions

  • #1

    Who can come?

    Anyone! People of all ages are welcome at the dance. Young children...please bring an adult.

  • #2

    What's the cost?

    $10. $5 if you help with setup or takedown, free if you do both or sit at the door for 30 minutes. First time dancers get in for free, except for the 1st Friday of the month.

  • #3

    What if I don't know how to dance?

    Not a problem. Come as you are, move as you like. We work hard to make it a safe and enoyable space.

  • #4

    Wait, where's this thingy again?

    The ILC (Integrative Learning Center) is located on North Pleasant Street, near the center of campus. If you can't find it, the address is 650 N Pleasant St, Amherst, MA 01002. See you there!
